Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers to Your Questions

How do I book a taxi?

You can book a taxi in multiple ways: use our online booking form on the website, call us at +91 9816608298, or message us on WhatsApp. All methods are quick and easy!

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 2 hours before the scheduled pickup time. Cancellations made within 2 hours may incur 20% of the booking amount.

Are your vehicles insured?

Yes, all our vehicles are fully insured and regularly maintained. Your safety is our top priority.

Can I book for a multi-day tour?

Absolutely! We offer customized multi-day tour packages. Contact us to discuss your requirements and get a personalized quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash payments, online bank transfers, Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and other digital payment methods. Payments can be made online or directly to the driver for your convenience.

Do you provide drivers for long distances?

Yes, we provide experienced drivers for long-distance trips. We have special rates for inter-state travels and highway journeys.
